Generate Playlist Using Auxo

Use Auxo to generate a new playlist on your Spotify account based on input factors


1. Sign in to Spotify
2. Scroll down and retrieve an OAuth Token by clicking "Get Token"
3. When prompted, select "playlist-modify-private" and "playlist-modify-public"

Go to your Spotify profile, and Right-click on your name, press Option and copy your Spotify URI
